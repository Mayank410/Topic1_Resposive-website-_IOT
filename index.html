Activity-1

  • What is an IoT Application?

    To put it simply, an IoT application refers to connecting different objects to one another and to humans through the internet. This connection enables the transfer of data between the objects and humans. The components of an IoT application may include: -

    • The process involves utilizing artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to connect sensor data to the cloud,.
    • enabling the real-time transmission of data for various applications, and performing data analysis.
    • An easy access with dashboard.
    • Furnishing instantaneous data for various objectives.
    • Analyzing the data.

  • What are the main features needed?

    The following features are required in an IoT Application: -

    • To ensure high-quality data collection, reliable and accurate sensors are crucial. Along with that
    • implementing effective cybersecurity measures is essential to protect the system from potential cyber-attacks
    • establishing a robust and reliable connectivity infrastructure is essential for seamless data transmission
    • Connectivity
    • A power outage can disrupt the data collection process and cause significant losses
    • it is essential to have a reliable power supply and backup systems in place to avoid such disruptions.

    Here are a few exaples of an IoT Application: -

    • Healthcare systems refer to the infrastructure and resources that are dedicated to the delivery of medical care to patients.
    • Smart escalators
    • An automatic or smart door is a type of door that is equipped with sensors or other devices that allow it to open and close automatically.
    • Alexa and Google Assistant are voice-activated digital assistants that use artificial intelligence to perform various tasks and provide information to users.
    • Alexa /Google assistant
    • Pacemakers
    • Smart escalators are escalators that are equipped with sensors and other technology that enable them to operate more efficiently and safely.
    • A smart phone is a mobile device that is equipped with advanced features and capabilities, such as mobile internet access, GPS navigation, high-quality cameras, and mobile apps.
